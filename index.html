Meet HappSchool Buddy

It’s a student experience chatbot which chats with your students and engages those who are unhappy, at risk, disengaged or have extra needs.

AI Buddy powered with social and emotional elements encourages students as they go through the learning process, makes learning fun and inclusive. AI Buddy reaches out to students in a personalised, timely manner, interprets sentiment, and responds empathetically, assisting them with tasks, schedules, deadlines. Gamified experience through gained points and completed goals contribute to the Happiness level of the AI Buddy. The better performance, the more happy and animated Buddy becomes. This motivates students to stay focused on their goals.

Teachers save time and are able to focus their efforts on individual students 

Teachers instantly get the picture of students’ emotional wellbeing, level of difficulty of the tasks, identify and alert in real-time any ‘at risk’ students who required immediate intervention and support. Chatbot is built into all lessons which saves teachers time in answering student questions on schedule, assignments, deadlines.

Parents get immediate and relevant feedback to support their child’s learning journey 

Parents get notified in real-time on their child’s progress in the classroom. They are informed of their child’s learning gaps which allows them to provide appropriate support to aid their learning.

Moreover, parents get immediate feedback from teachers on their child’s progress. HappSchool provides relevant and real-time data that enables immediate intervention and support to students

Better Understanding Individual Students  

HappSchool is an absolute game-changer for teachers. It is a streamlined way for kids to show their thinking and understanding in ways that teachers usually don’t see.

Which students are struggling and what do they need help with? Actionable data insights to teachers and school leaders helps to effectively support students learning where they need it most.

HappSchool offers the ability to interrogate and understand the nuanced cognitive, emotional, and motivational processes that shape education in any learning environment – blended or remote.
